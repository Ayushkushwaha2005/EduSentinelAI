EduSentinel AI is one account across every product in the ecosystem — the
browser extension, the mobile app, the learning hub, and everything that
follows. You sign up once.

## 1. Create your account

Go to [Sign up](/signup) and register with your email address. You will
receive a verification link; it expires in 24 hours.

## 2. Turn on two-factor authentication

From your dashboard, open **Security** and set up two-factor
authentication with any authenticator app (Google Authenticator, 1Password,
Aegis, and others all work).

This is optional for regular accounts and **mandatory** for administrator
accounts. We recommend it for everyone: a password alone is one leak away
from being useless.

## 3. Install a product

Visit the [Download Center](/downloads). Every artifact there is published
with its SHA-256 checksum and signed with our release key.

Before installing anything, verify it:

```bash
sha256sum <downloaded-file>
```

Compare the result to the checksum shown on the page. If it does not match,
do not install the file — report it to security@edusentinel.ai.

## 4. If something goes wrong

- Lost your password? Use [Forgot password](/forgot-password). Resetting it
  signs you out of every device.
- Think your account is compromised? Sign in, open **Security**, and choose
  **Sign out everywhere**, then change your password.
- Found a vulnerability? See our
  [Security & Disclosure policy](/legal/security).
